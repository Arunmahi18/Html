French Fries Receipt

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Cold water
  • Red chiili powder
  • Salt
  • Rose berry
  1. Wash and peel the skin of potatoes.
  2. Cut them into 1/2 inch thick slices and then into thin strips of uniforms size.
  3. Wash the strips in plain water or ice water for 2-3 times to remove the excess strach content.
  4. Boil 2 cups of water in a pan.
  5. Switch off the flame and soak the potato strips in it for 5 minutes. No need to cover the bowl.
  6. Drain the water completely.
  7. Pat it dry in aclean kitchen towel.
  8. Leave it open for 5 minutes. Make sure there is no water in the in the strips and it should be completely dry.
  9. Heat oil till medium hot and deep fry the potato strips for 2 minutes in medium flame. It would be oily and soft.
  10. Make sure the color of potatoes are white. Remove & Drain them in a tissue paper. Let it cool down completely.
  11. Again heat oil till its well heated but not smokey.
  12. Now deep fry the potato srtips for the second time till it becomes crispy and golden.
  13. Keep tossing them for uniform cooking.
  14. Remove in atissue and spinkle some salt. Serve with tomato sauce.
